About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Columbia University (New York, USA). My research lies at the intersection of ecohydrology and data-driven Earth system science, focusing on evapotranspiration, water resources, extreme events, and hybrid physics–ML modeling. I previously held a postdoctoral position at the Max Planck Institute for Biogeochemistry and received my Ph.D. in Environmental Science from Peking University. Feel free to reach me by email.
